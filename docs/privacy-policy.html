SpeedWall Overlay — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

SpeedWall Overlay does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no internet connection required.

Device Permissions

  • Camera — Used solely to display a live camera feed for overlay calibration. No images are recorded or transmitted.
  • Photo Library — Used only when you choose to save a screenshot. Images are saved locally to your device.
  • Motion Sensors — Used for the optional auto-level feature. Motion data is processed on-device and never stored or shared.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, and no third-party services.

Third-Party Services

The app uses no third-party SDKs, frameworks, or services that collect data.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
